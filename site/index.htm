    exercises

    At Signature ball, We care about your health and well being, this is why we will provide you a wide range of exercises to do at home or at a gym. You will have fun. I will give you routine exercises done in soccer by professionals . I encourage you to try these exercises even if you don't play soccer or any sport for that matter.

    Examples: push-ups

    To complete this exercise you will need to lay down on the floor flat, while then bending your arms close to your chest, the palm of your hands flat on the ground while keeping your legs straight and closed to each other, next push your body upward with you hands only until your arms are all stretched out while still keeping your legs straight, then return to the ground by bending your arms half way. Repeat the task multiple time to gain, arm muscle, and chest muscle.
